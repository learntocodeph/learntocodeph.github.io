Introduction to Cell

  • All life is made up of cells
  • They come in thousands of shapes and sizes
  • The human has over 100 trillion cells, carrying out the dance of life
  • They are tiny that 10,000 could fit on the head of a pin
  • The cells in our body undergo over 500 quadrillion chemical reactions every second
  • Their amazing biochemistry has nurtures life on Earth for billions of years and protected us from harm

OTHER INFORMATIONS

  • Cells that have cell membrane (Plant, Blood cells and Animals)
  • Cell organelles (ex: mitochondria, chloroplast, nucleus)
  • What controls a cell- Nucleus
  • The powerhouse of the cell- Mitochondria
  • Function of cell membrane (it controls substances entering and leaving the cell)
  • Cytoplasm- a gelatin substance that fills a cell
  • Nerve cell- to carry nervous impulses around the body
  • Muscle cells- cells that produce movement
